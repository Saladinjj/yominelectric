Company Introduction

Zhejiang Yomin Electric Co., Ltd.

Founded in 2005 and headquartered in Zhejiang Province, China's hub of electrical manufacturing excellence, Yomin Electric has grown from a local manufacturer into a globally trusted supplier of precision energy metering products.

Our 18,000 m² production facility houses state-of-the-art automated assembly lines, a dedicated R&D center, and a comprehensive quality control laboratory. Every product that leaves our factory undergoes rigorous testing against international standards.

With a team of over 280 engineers and technicians, we serve utility companies, contractors, distributors, and OEM partners across Asia, Africa, Europe, the Middle East, and the Americas.

Company Timeline

Two Decades of Growth

From a small workshop to a global exporter — our journey in electrical manufacturing.

Company Founded

Yomin Electric established in Wenzhou, Zhejiang Province. First production line for single-phase energy meters launched.

2005
2008

ISO 9001 Certification

Achieved ISO 9001:2008 quality management certification. Began exporting to Southeast Asia and Middle East markets.

CE Certification & European Entry

Obtained CE marking for full product range. First major European distribution contracts signed with partners in Germany and France.

2011
2015

New Manufacturing Campus

Moved to an 18,000 m² purpose-built facility with automated assembly lines, dedicated R&D lab, and expanded quality control center.

Smart Gateway Product Line

Launched IoT-enabled smart gateway products for AMR and AMI system integration. Partnered with utility companies in 12 new countries.

2019
2024

95+ Countries, 12M+ Units

Reached milestones of serving over 95 countries and shipping more than 12 million units. Ongoing R&D investment in next-generation smart meters.
