Our Solutions

End-to-End Metering Technology

Precision Energy Metering

We design and manufacture a complete range of static energy meters compliant with IEC 62053, IEC 61036, and ANSI C12 standards. Suitable for residential, commercial and industrial applications in over 95 countries.

  • Single-phase & three-phase designs
  • Class 0.2S, 0.5S, 1 & 2 accuracy
  • DIN rail, panel & socket mounting
  • Wide voltage & frequency range

Smart Grid & IoT Integration

Our smart meters and data concentrators enable Advanced Metering Infrastructure (AMI) deployment. RS485 MODBUS, Zigbee, GPRS, and LoRa communication options ensure compatibility with any head-end system.

  • RS485 / MODBUS RTU protocol
  • Zigbee & LoRa wireless options
  • Data Concentrator Units (DCU)
  • Remote reading & load control

Prepaid Metering Systems

Complete keypad and smart card prepaid metering solutions for utility revenue protection and energy access programs. Supports STS token systems widely used in Africa, Asia, and the Middle East.

  • STS-compliant token generation
  • Keypad & IC card options
  • Anti-tamper & fraud detection
  • Remote top-up capability
